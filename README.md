This project involves a comprehensive analysis of a customer dataset to identify key factors influencing bike purchases. By cleaning the data and building an interactive dashboard using Microsoft Excel, I uncovered trends related to **age**, **income**, and **commute distance**.

## Technical Implementation
- I used Excel to clean the data, remove duplicates, and standardize data formats.
- I created a custom nested IF formula to categorize ages into **Young Adult**, **Middle Age**, and **Old** for better demographic segmentation.
- I Used **Pivot Tables** to aggregate complex data points across multiple dimensions.
- I designed a user-friendly dashboard with **Slicers** for real-time data filtering by **Marital Status**, **Region**,and **Education** and customized charts to reduce cognitive load.

## Key Analytical Insights
- Customers with a commute of **0-1 miles** are the most likely to purchase a bike. Purchases drop by nearly 80% when the commute exceeds **10 miles**.
- The **Middle Age** group (31-54) group represents the largest customer base, significantly outperforming Young Adults and Old  in total **Yes** responses.
- There is a positive correlation between higher average income and the likelihood of purchasing a bike, particularly among male customers.
